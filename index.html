Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 3, 2026

NutriLens ("we", "us", or "our") respects your privacy. When it comes to your personal information, we believe in transparency, not surprises. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your choices and rights.

By using NutriLens, you confirm you have read and understood this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information when you use NutriLens:

  • Camera & image data — Photos of food you scan for nutritional analysis. Images are processed for recognition and are not stored on our servers without your consent.
  • Nutrition & health data — Food logs, dietary goals, and preferences you voluntarily enter into the app.
  • Usage data — How you interact with the app, which features you use, and performance metrics.
  • Device information — Device type, operating system version, and identifiers used for crash reporting and diagnostics.
  • Account information — If you create an account, your email address and display name.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide food recognition and nutritional analysis features
  • Personalize your nutrition tracking experience
  • Improve app performance and fix bugs
  • Send optional meal reminders or nutrition summaries, if you opt in
  • Comply with applicable laws and regulations

We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

3. Third-Party Services

NutriLens uses the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

  • Google Firebase — Analytics, crash reporting, and authentication
  • Google ML Kit — On-device food and image recognition
  • OpenAI / Gemini API — AI-powered nutritional insights (queries are anonymized)

We encourage you to review the privacy policies of these services.

4. App Permissions

NutriLens may request the following device permissions:

  • Camera (required) — To scan and photograph food items for analysis
  • Photo Library / Storage (optional) — To upload photos from your gallery
  • Notifications (optional) — For meal reminders and daily summaries

All optional permissions can be revoked at any time in your device settings.

5. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures including HTTPS encryption for all data transmission and secure storage practices. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Data Retention & Deletion

We retain your personal data only as long as necessary to provide our services or as required by law. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us.

7. Children's Privacy

NutriLens is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of analytics or marketing communications

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details below.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the effective date above. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
