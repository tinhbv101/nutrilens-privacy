Privacy Policy

📋 Overview

NutriLens ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the NutriLens mobile application.

By using NutriLens, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

📦 Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Camera data: Images of food items you scan to analyze nutritional content. Images are processed locally or via our API and are not stored on our servers without your explicit consent.
  • Usage data: App interactions, features used, and performance data to improve the app experience.
  • Device information: Device type, operating system version, and unique device identifiers for crash reporting and analytics.
  • Health & nutrition data: Food logs, dietary preferences, and nutritional goals you voluntarily enter.
  • Account information (if applicable): Email address and display name if you create an account.

🎯 How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide food recognition and nutritional analysis features
  • Personalize your nutrition tracking experience
  • Improve app performance and fix bugs
  • Send optional notifications (e.g., meal reminders) if enabled
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

🔗 Third-Party Services

NutriLens may use the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Google Firebase – Analytics, crash reporting, authentication
  • Google ML Kit / Vision API – On-device food recognition
  • OpenAI / Gemini API – AI-powered nutritional insights (anonymized queries only)

These services may collect data as described in their respective privacy policies. We encourage you to review them.

📷 Camera & Permissions

NutriLens requests the following device permissions:

  • Camera: Required to scan and photograph food items for nutritional analysis.
  • Photo Library / Storage: To allow you to upload existing photos from your gallery.
  • Notifications (optional): For meal reminders and daily nutrition summaries.

All permissions are optional unless stated otherwise and can be revoked in your device settings.

🔒 Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including HTTPS encryption for all data transmission and secure storage practices. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

👶 Children's Privacy

NutriLens is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately.

🌍 Data Retention & Deletion

We retain your personal data only as long as necessary to provide our services or as required by law. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us.

✏️ Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date below. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.
